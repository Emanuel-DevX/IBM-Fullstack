About Us

TravelBloom was founded on the belief that travel should be meaningful, accessible, and inspiring. We curate recommendations not just from data—but from a passion for discovering the unknown. Built by Emanuel Fisha Molla as part of the IBM Full Stack Developer program, this app showcases a blend of modern technology and wanderlust.

Behind every recommendation lies a story—our aim is to help you write yours. Our mission is to empower every traveler to explore the world, one destination at a time, using intelligent tools and personalized guidance.
